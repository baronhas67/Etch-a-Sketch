How to play

This is a very minimal Etch-a-Sketch. Here is how to play:

  • Click new size, enter a size. Max is 100
  • The default color is blue, change it with new color button
  • The rgb button makes the brush produce red, gree and blue colors at random
  • The enable rgb left click the rgb button. To disable it right click the same
  • To reset just click new size again
  • More colors is just a more powerful version of rgb
  • In custom colors you can add as many as colors you want and it will randomize when you paint
  • Clicking on another mode or button will disable others
  • You can also right click on a button to disable it
  • If you enter a wrong color name, The paint will not be displayed
  • You can also enter hex codes
  • Try some color combinations like gold, black, white
I hope you enjoy it, we would like your feedback.
